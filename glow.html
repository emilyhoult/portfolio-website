Glow

Positivity App

I came up with the idea for this app after attending a training on Positive Psychology and noticing a gap in the market for this kind of app, especially considering the current influx of negativity in the news. The focus of this project was to design an app for both iOS and Material Design, and I used Adobe XD to design.
